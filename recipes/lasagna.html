Lasagna

Description

It's everybodys favorite dinner, LASAGNA

Ingredients

    Lasagna sauce

  • 2 onions
  • 4 garlic
  • 400 grams minced meat
  • 2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried timian
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 squash
  • 5 leafs of selleri
  • 1 dl concentrated tomato pure
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 dl red wine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt
  • pebber

    Mornay sauce

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3 dl milk
  • 1 knifesedge of muskatnut
  • 125 grams fresh mozzarella

    Additionally

  • 200 grams lasagna plates
  • 125 grams fresh mozzarella

Steps

    Lasagna sauce

  1. Saute onions and garlic in olive until they are see through
  2. Add the meat and brown it at high heat. Turn down the heat and add the rest of the ingredients
  3. Let the sauce simmer under a lid for 2-6 hours the longer, the better. Taste the sauce periodically and adjust the seasoning with more spices, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes to taste and finally, salt and pepper. If you think it becomes too dry, you can add more vegetable broth, but it's best to wait until it has simmered for about an hour, as the vegetables will also release some liquid into the sauce.

    4. Mornay sauce

  4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the flour, and whisk until it forms a ball. Slowly whisk in the milk, a little at a time, until the consistency is like a sauce. Add the cheese and whisk until it has melted. Season with grated nutmeg, salt, and pepper to taste.

    5. Lasagna

  5. Spread a layer of Mornay sauce in a greased ovenproof dish. Then, alternate layers of the lasagna sauce and lasagna sheets in the dish, finishing with a layer of sauce. Top with a final layer of Mornay sauce and thinly sliced mozzarella. Place the dish in a preheated oven at 175Â°C (convection) and bake the lasagna for about 20-25 minutes, until the lasagna sheets are tender with a slight bite.
