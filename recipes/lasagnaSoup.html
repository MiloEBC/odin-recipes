Description

It's everybodys favorite LASAGNASOUP baby

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 400 grams minced meat
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 leafselleri
  • 1 squash
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons concentrated tomato pure
  • 2 dl vegetable boullion
  • 4 lasagnaplates, snapped in squares
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • salt
  • black pebber

For serving

  • 125 grams fresh mozzarella
  • 50 grams parmesan
  • 1 handfull fresh timian

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saute onions and garlic until the onions are soft
  2. Increase the heat and add the meat to the pot. Brown the meat well while stirring, then add carrots, celery, and zucchini along with chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and vegetable broth.
  3. Let it simmer for 15â€“30 minutes. Add lasagna sheets by pushing them into the sauce with a bit of space between the sheets so they don't stick together. Let the lasagna sheets cook for 10 minutes, or until they are perfectly tender.
  4. Taste and adjust with salt and pepper, and adjust the consistency with more vegetable broth if you prefer a more "soupy" texture.
  5. Serve in deep bowls with a bit of fresh mozzarella stirred into the lasagna soup, some freshly grated Parmesan on top, and a sprinkle of fresh thyme leaves.
